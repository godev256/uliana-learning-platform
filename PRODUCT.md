# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Parents and school students looking for a private mathematics tutor for OGE, EGE, school program support, or stronger mathematical confidence. They arrive with a practical need: understand the tutor's method quickly, assess trust and seriousness, and find a clear way to book an introductory diagnosis.

## Product Purpose

This is a personal brand site for Uliana, a mathematics tutor. It should communicate calm expertise, individual attention, and exam readiness, then move visitors toward a first diagnostic conversation.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, gallery-like. The tone should feel controlled and confident rather than promotional, colorful, or school-themed.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic education landing pages with bright blue/green palettes, sticky top navigation, oversized hero slogans, stock illustrations, emoji, colorful icons, heavy card grids, gradients, and soft drop shadows. Avoid turning the tutor into an ecommerce product; the gallery reference should shape restraint, whitespace, and rhythm.

## Design Principles

1. Treat the tutor portrait as the central artifact, with generous white space around it.
2. Build hierarchy through position, tracking, tone, and whitespace, not type weight or color.
3. Keep every interaction quiet and purposeful, with motion that feels editorial rather than decorative.
4. Make the booking path obvious without introducing loud CTA color.
5. Preserve accessibility: semantic sections, visible focus, readable contrast, reduced-motion behavior.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, keep keyboard focus visible, provide meaningful image alt text, and keep all navigation links reachable without pointer-only interactions.
